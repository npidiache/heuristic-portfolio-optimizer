the problem

A stagnation trigger its own budget can't reach

A bee scouts when its failed-trial counter exceeds max_trials. The calibration set the limit by problem dimension — 0.6 × 25 × 20 = 300 — but counters accrue with the iteration horizon (60). Proportional limits tied to the horizon (9, 15) fire at measured rates; the calibrated 300 never does. The contingency system was calibrated out of existence.

the discovery

Activation is necessary — not sufficient

Every adaptive variant wakes the scout, but only one beats the dormant baseline. Same activation rate, different outcome: the move geometry — not the firing frequency — is the differentiator.

the verdict

Formal control: Holm within each cell, deflated Sharpe on the winner

Deflated Sharpe of the winner's best portfolio per cell — its daily-return Sharpe deflated against all seven configurations' trials (Bailey & López de Prado, 2014). Threshold tick at 0.95.

Hypothesis: replacing the dormant scout with the Dirichlet elite restart improves on ABC original. Result: the relative claim survives formal control — significant and in the winner's favor in 8/8 cells after Holm. The absolute claim is regime-dependent: DSR ≥ 0.95 in the COVID and stability cells (up to 0.9998), but 0.51–0.72 in GFC and war windows, where even the winning portfolio's absolute Sharpe is too small to distinguish skill from best-of-seven luck.
Diagnostics, not thesis results. In-sample windows under frozen thesis conventions; uncalibrated policy defaults; deflation covers the seven configurations tested, not the full design space. Confirmatory step: the SG-5 walk-forward study on the 2000–2025 panel. Frozen thesis artifacts untouched — Tier-1/2/3 guards green.
